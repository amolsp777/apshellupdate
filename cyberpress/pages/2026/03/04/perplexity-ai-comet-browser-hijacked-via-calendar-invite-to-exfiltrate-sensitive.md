Security researchers at Zenity Labs uncovered a critical vulnerability in Perplexity’s Comet “agentic” browser, enabling attackers to steal sensitive local files through a weaponized Google Calendar invite. Dubbed “PerplexedBrowser” and part of Zenity’s “PleaseFix” family, the flaw affected Comet on macOS, Windows, and Android. Rated P1 (critical) on Bugcrow
