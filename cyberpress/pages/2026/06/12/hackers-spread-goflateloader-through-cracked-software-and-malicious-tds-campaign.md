Sometimes the most effective malware relies on blunt force rather than technical elegance. GoFlateLoader is a simple, Go-based in-memory loader that deliberately inflates its file size to bypass security scanners. Despite lacking advanced anti-debugging, anti-VM, or sandbox-evasion techniques, it has successfully targeted over 33,000 users since April 2026.
