A coalition of U.S. federal agencies, including CISA, the FBI, and the NSA, has issued an urgent joint advisory regarding active cyberattacks targeting Automatic Tank Gauge (ATG) systems. These critical operational technology (OT) assets provide automated, remote monitoring of storage tank parameters, including fuel levels, temperatures, and leak detection.
