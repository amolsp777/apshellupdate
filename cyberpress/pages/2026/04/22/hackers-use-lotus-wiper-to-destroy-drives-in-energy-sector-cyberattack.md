Kaspersky researchers have uncovered a previously undocumented destructive malware campaign targeting Venezuela’s energy and utilities sector, deploying a new wiper dubbed Lotus Wiper that permanently destroys data across physical drives with no recovery possible. Unlike ransomware, the attack carries no extortion component its sole purpose is annihilation.
